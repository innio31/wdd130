Ogun, Nigeria

Ogun State is a state in southwestern Nigeria. Its capital is Abeokuta, and its motto is "Gateway to Nigeria (Ipinle Ekun)." It was created on February 3, 1976, from the former Western State. Ogun State shares borders with Oyo State to the north, Ondo State to the east, Lagos State to the south, and the Republic of Benin to the west. The state has a population of over 5 million people and is known for its rich cultural heritage, including traditional festivals, music, and art.
