Rapid Waters Rafting

Our Mission

At Rapid Waters Rafting, our mission is to provide safe, exhilarating, and unforgettable white water rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe in fostering a deep appreciation for nature while pushing the boundaries of adventure.

Our experienced guides are passionate about river conservation and dedicated to creating memorable experiences that emphasize safety, teamwork, and respect for the powerful forces of nature.

History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiast Michael "Rapid Mike" Johnson, Rapid Waters Rafting began as a small operation with just two rafts and a dream. What started as weekend adventures for friends quickly grew into a reputable rafting company known for its safety standards and exceptional guides.

Over the past 25+ years, we've introduced over 50,000 adventurers to the thrill of white water rafting. We've expanded our operations to include five major rivers across the region, each offering unique challenges and breathtaking scenery.

Our commitment to environmental stewardship has remained constant throughout our growth. We actively participate in river conservation efforts and follow Leave No Trace principles on all our expeditions.
